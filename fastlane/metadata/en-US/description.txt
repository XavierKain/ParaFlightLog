ParaFlightStats is your ultimate paragliding companion. Record your flights, track your statistics and manage your gear.

KEY FEATURES:
• Automatic flight recording with GPS
• Statistics tracking (flight time, altitude, distance)
• Wing and equipment management
• Apple Watch synchronization
• Widget for quick access
• Data export and backup

APPLE WATCH:
Start and track your flights directly from your wrist with the dedicated Apple Watch app.

WIDGETS:
Keep an eye on your statistics with home screen widgets.
